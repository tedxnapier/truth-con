TruthCon is a TEDxNapier Bridge impact project that explores the world of misinformation by examining its origins, its appeal, and how it spreads - supercharged by AI - and solutions to it.
The microsite developed as part of the project offers citizens solutions in the form of a reporting mechanism to counter misinformation, including deepfakes.
